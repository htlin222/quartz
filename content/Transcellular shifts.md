# Transcellular shifts

* Alkalemia,
* insulin,
* catecholamines
* [[β2-agonists]]
* hypothermia
* hypokalemic/thyrotoxic periodic paralysis,
* acute ↑ [[hematopoiesis]] (megaloblastic anemia Rx w/ B12, AML crisis),
* [[chloroquine]]

## overdose:
* Ba/Cs,
* antipsychotics (risperidone, quetiapine)
* theophylline
