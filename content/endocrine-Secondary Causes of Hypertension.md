# endocrine-Secondary Causes of Hypertension

## Hyperaldo or Cushing’s (1–5%)

- Hypokalemia
- Metabolic alkalosis
- See “Adrenal Disorders” [[adrenal disorders]]

## Pheochromocytoma (<1%)

- Paroxysmal HTN, H/A, palp.

## Myxedema (<1%)

- See “Thyroid Disorders” [[thyroid disorders]]
- TFTs

## Hypercalcemia (<1%)

- Polyuria, dehydration, ∆ MS
- iCa
