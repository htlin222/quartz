# Peritoneal dialysis-associated-Bacterial peritonitis

cloudy fluid, abd pain, fever, nausea. Dx can be made with >50 PMNs. Culture most often GPC (50%) or GNR (15%).

Rx: vanc + gent (IV load, then administer in PD).
