# Pel-Ebstein

這是一種特殊類型的發燒，在 3-10 天的發熱期和隨後相同長度的無發熱期之間循環。

Pel-Ebstein fever is a rare condition reported in patients with Hodgkin's lymphoma, characterized by cyclic fevers that rise and fall every one or two weeks.
