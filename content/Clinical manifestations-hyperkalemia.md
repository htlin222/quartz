# Clinical manifestations-hyperkalemia

- Weakness, nausea, paresthesias, palpitations; Renal: ↓ NH4+ secretion → acidosis

- ECG:
  - ST depression,
  - peaked T waves,
  - ↓ QT,
  - ↑ PR interval,
  - ↑ QRS width,
  - loss of P wave,
  - sine wave pattern,
  - PEA/VF (ECG: low sens.,
  - cardiac arrest can be first manifestation!)
