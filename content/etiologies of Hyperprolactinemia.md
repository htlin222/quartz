# etiologies of Hyperprolactinemia

- Physiologic hypersecretion
  - Pregnancy
  - Lactation
  - Chest wall stimulation
  - Sleep
  - Stress
- [[Hypothalamic-pituitary stalk damage]]
- Pituitary hypersecretion

  - prolactinoma
  - [[Acromegaly]]

- Systemic disorders
  - Chronicrenal failure
  - Hypothyroidism
  - Cirrhosis
  - Pseudocyesis
  - Epileptic seizures
- [[drug-induced hyperprolactinemia]]
