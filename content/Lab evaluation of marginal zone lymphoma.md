# Lab evaluation of marginal zone lymphoma

* CBC w/ diff,
* CMP,
* LDH,
* HBV sAg/cAb,
* HCV Ab,
* HCV PCR,
* HIV,
* SPEP/IFE (paraprotein often present),
* quant immunoglobulins
