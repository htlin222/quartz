## Evaluation-atrial fibrillation

- H&P, ECG, CXR, TTE (LA size, thrombus, valves, LV fxn, pericardium),
- K, Mg, Cr, TFTs
- In acute AF <48º, ~70% spont. convert to SR w/in 48 hrs (NEJM 2019;380:1499)
