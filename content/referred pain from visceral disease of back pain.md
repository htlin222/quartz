# referred pain from visceral disease of back pain

## GI:
* PUD
* cholelithiasis
* pancreatitis
* pancreatic cancer

## GU: pyelonephritis
* nephrolithiasis
* uterine or ovarian cancer
* salpingitis

## Vascular:
* aortic dissection
* leaking aortic aneurysm
