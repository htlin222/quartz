# Clinical presentation-of-renal-cell-carcinoma

- Mostly asx & incidental on imaging
  - (minority will present w/ flank pain, hematuria, pyrexia, cachexia, fatigue)
- At presentation:
  - 65% localized dz,
  - 16% locally advanced,
  - 16% met
