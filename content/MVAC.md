# MVAC

- Methotrexate 30 mg/m2 IV Push
- Vinblastine 3 or 4 mg/m2 IV Push
- [[Doxorubicin]] 30 mg/m2 IV Push
- [[Cisplatin]] 50 or 70 mg/m2 IVF over 60 mins (rate 180ml/hr at KFSYSCC)
