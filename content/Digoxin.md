# Digoxin

- 0.5 then 0.25 mg q6h
- up to 1.5 mg/24 h
- 0.125-0.375 mg
- qd (adjust for CrCl)
- Consider in HF or low BP
- Poor exertional HR ctrl

- 23% ↓ HF hosp., no ∆ mort (NEJM 1997;336:525); ? ↑ mort w/ ↑ levels (NEJM 2002;347:1403); optimal 0.5–0.8 ng/mL (JAMA 2003;289:871)
