# ABC Score #🚧 施工中

* Penetrating mechanism (Yes = 1, No = 0)
* Systolic BP ≤ 90 mmHg in the Emergency Department (Yes = 1, No = 0)
* HR ≥ 120 in the Emergency Department (Yes = 1, No = 0)
* Positive FAST Ultrasound (Yes = 1, No = 0)
