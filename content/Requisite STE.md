# Requisite STE

必要的

- ≥2 contiguous leads w/ ≥1 mm (except for V2–V3: ≥2 mm in ♂ and ≥1.5 mm in ♀), or
- New or presumed new LBBB w/ compelling H&P, or
- True posterior MI: ST depression V1–V3 ± tall Rw w/ STE on posterior leads (V7–V9)
