# Diagnosis of autoimmune pancreatitis

- Labs:
  - cholestatic LFTs (↑ Aϕ >AST/ALT),
  - ↑ γ-globulins and IgG4,
  - ⊕ ANA,
  - RF
- HISORt criteria:
  - Histology,
  - Imaging (“[[sausage pancreas]],” bile duct stricture),
  - Serology,
  - other Organ involvement,
  - Response to therapy
