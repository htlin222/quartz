# Treatment-hyperaldosteronism

(SCNA 2014;94:643)
* Adenoma → adrenalectomy vs. medical Rx w/ spironolactone or eplerenone
* Hyperplasia → spironolactone or eplerenone; GRA → glucocorticoids ± spironolactone
* Carcinoma → adrenalectomy
