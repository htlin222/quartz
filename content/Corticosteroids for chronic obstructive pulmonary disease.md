# Corticosteroids for chronic obstructive pulmonary disease

* (inhaled, ICS): 
 * ~11% ↓ in exacerbations & slows ↓ FEV1;
* no Δ in mortality 
* (Lancet 2016;387:1817). 
* Greatest benefit if eos >300 (Lancet Repir Med 2018;6:117).
