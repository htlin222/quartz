# Granulocytopenia or neutropenia

(includes DM, ESRD → functional impairment)

- Bacteria:
  - Gram positive:
    - coag ⊖ staph,
    - S. aureus,
    - viridans strep,
    - S. pneumo, other strep;
  - Corynebacterium spp., Bacillus spp.
  - Gram negative:
    - E. coli,
    - Klebsiella,
    - Pseudomonas
- Fungi:
  - Yeast:
    - Candida albicans and other Candida spp.
  - Molds:
    - Aspergillus,
    - Mucor spp.,
    - endemic fungi and others
- Viruses:
  - VZV,
  - HSV1 and 2,
  - CMV
