# Molecular classification of Colon Cancer

> Consensus molecular subtypes of colon CA:

- CMS1
  - MSI Immune,
  - hypermutated,
  - immune infiltrated,
  - BRAF V600E mut
- CMS2
  - canonical/epithelial,
  - MSS,
  - CIN,
  - Wnt/Myc activation,
  - EGFR dependent
- CMS3
  - metabolic,
  - CIN-MSI mixed,
  - metabolic dysregulation,
  - KRAS mut
- CMS4
  - mesenchymal,
  - MSS,
  - CIN,
  - activated TGFb,
  - & angiogenesis pathway,
  - immune tolerant,
  - worst prognosis
