# definition and epidemiology of multiple myeloma (MM)

(Lancet 2021;397:410)

- Malignant neoplasm of plasma cells
	producing a monoclonal Ig = “M protein”
- ~27,000 new cases/y;
- median age at diagnosis 69 y; more common in African Americans
