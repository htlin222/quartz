# Secondary immune thrombocytopenic purpura (2° ITP)

Diagnosis:

- viral serologies (HIV, HCV, HBV, EBV),
- H. pylori Ab, ANA, pregnancy test,
- APLA,
- TSH,
- parvovirus, & CMV PCR.
- Anti-plt Ab tests often sent but less useful.
