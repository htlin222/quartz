# Ddx-of-dominant-R-wave-in-V1-or-V2

- Ventricular abnl:
  - RVH (RAD, RAA, deep S waves in I, V5, V6);
  - HCM;
  - Duchenne’s
- Posterior MI:
  - anterior R wave = posterior Q wave;
  - often with IMI
- Abnormal depolarization:
  - RBBB (QRS >120 msec, rSR′);
  - WPW (↓ PR, δ wave, ↑ QRS)
- Other:
  - dextroversion;
  - counterclockwise rotation;
  - lead misplacement;
  - nl variant
