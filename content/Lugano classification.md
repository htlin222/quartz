# Lugano classification

- Lugano classification (JCO 2014; 32:3059-67).

## Limited

- Stage I (one node or group of adjacent nodes), 一顆或幾顆連在
- Stage II (two or more nodal groups), 兩群以上
- IIB (“bulky” dsx). 大
