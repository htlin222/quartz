# Viral diarrhea

### Rotavirus

Outbreak person to person (PTP), daycare; lasts 4–8 d.

### Norovirus
* ~50% of all diarrhea.
* Winter outbreaks;
* PTP & food/water;
* no immunity.
* Lasts 1–3 d.
* Vomiting prominent.
