# Candida species

- Microbiology: normal GI flora; C. albicans & nonalbicans spp.

- Epidemiology:

  - neutropenia, immunosupp.,
  - broad-spectrum abx,
  - intravascular catheters (esp. if TPN),
  - IVDU,
  - abd surgery,
  - DM,
  - renal failure, age >65
