# HAS-BLED

- Hypertension
- Abnormal renal & liver function
- Stroke
- Bleeding
- Labile INRs
- Elderly (>65 y/o)
- Drugs & alcohol

## 低出血風險，Warfarin, INR 2.0-3.0 (target 2.5)

- Dabigatran 150 mg BID
- Rivaroxaban 20 mg QD

## 3 項以上: 高出血風險，密切監测，姐免其他出血展臉

- Dabigatran 110mg BID
- Rivaroxaban 15 mg QD
