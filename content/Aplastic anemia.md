# Aplastic anemia

(stem cell failure) (NEJM 2015;373:35)

## Epidemiology

- 2-5 cases/106/y
- biphasic (major peak in adolescents, 2nd peak in elderly)

## Diagnosis

pancytopenia w/

- ↓ retics,
- BM bx w/ hypocellularity, usually nl cytogenetics
