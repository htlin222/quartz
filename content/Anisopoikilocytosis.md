# Anisopoikilocytosis

- Anisocytosis - cells of different sizes
- Poikilocytosis - cells of different shapes

- Non-specific if mild
- Myelofibrosis
- Congenital and acquired dyserythropoietic anaemias
- Hereditary pyropoikilocytosis
  - HbH disease
