# Thalassemias

[[Mentzer index]]

- Hb 的 α 或 β 珠蛋白鏈的合成 ↓
- 溶血性貧血和無效造血的發生

(Lancet 2018;391:155)

- ↓ synthesis of α- or β-globin chains of Hb → ≠ subunits → destruction of RBCs and erythroid precursors; ∴ anemia from hemolysis and ineffective erythropoiesis
