# Stages I–II Hodgkin lymphoma

* PET adapted ABVD
	* doxorubicin,
	* bleomycin,
	* vinblastine,
	* dacarbazine
± RT

## Favorable:

ABVD × 2–4 cycles ± RT
(if PET ⊖ after 2 cycles reduce chemo or RT)


## Unfavorable:

* >50 y, bulky mediastinum,
* “B” sx,
* ESR >50,
* >3 nodal sites

ABVD × 4–6 + RT (stop at 4 cycles if PET ⊖ after 2 cycles)
