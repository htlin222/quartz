# Pott’s disease

- 最危險的 → 肌肉骨骼 - 結核病，
  - 因 為: 它可導致骨質破壞、畸形和截癱。
- 最常: 胸椎 - 和 - 腰骶椎。
- Pott disease, or tuberculosis of the spine,
- has recently shown a significant resurgence in developed nations secondary to global migration.
