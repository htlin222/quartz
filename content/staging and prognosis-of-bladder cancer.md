# staging and prognosis-of-bladder cancer

- Early-stage prognostic factors:
  - Stage,
  - histologic grade,
  - presence of multicentric dz,
  - time to recurrence,
  - tumor size,
  - presence of carcinoma in situ (CIS)
- Met prognostic factors:
  - Poor performance status,
  - visceral mets
