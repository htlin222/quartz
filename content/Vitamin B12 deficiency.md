# Vitamin B12 deficiency

(NEJM 2013;368:149)

* B12 present only in foods of animal origin; total body stores sufficient for 2–3 y

* Binds to intrinsic factor (IF) secreted by gastric parietal cells; absorbed in terminal ileum
