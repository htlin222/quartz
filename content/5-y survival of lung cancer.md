# 5-y survival of lung cancer

* ~70–90% for stage I,
* 50–60% stage II,
* 15–35% stage III,
* 0–10% stage IV (J Thorac Oncol 2016;11:39);
* survival improving with newer therapies (NEJM 2020;383:640).
