# symptom to diagnosis of dyspnea

## Four most common
* Heart failure
* Pneumonia 
* Obstructive lung disease (chronic obstructive pulmonary disease/asthma) 
* Pulmonary embolism

## Immediate threat to life (ACT ASAP) (大概就是5T)
* Arrhythmia 
* Coronary syndrome, 
* Acute Tamponade 
* Airway obstruction Stenosis, 
* Aortic Anaphylaxis 
* Pneumothorax
