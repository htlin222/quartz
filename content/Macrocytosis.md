# Macrocytosis

- Macrocytosis - cells which are larger than a lymphocyte nucleus

## Causes

- Pregnancy
- [[Hypothyroidism]]
- Alcohol use
- Drugs: azathioprine, hydroxyurea
- B12/folate deficiency
- [[Reticulocytosis]]
- [[Imatinib]] / [[sunitinib]] therapy
- [[MDS]]
- Liver disease
