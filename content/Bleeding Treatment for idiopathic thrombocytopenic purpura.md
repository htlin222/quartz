# Bleeding Treatment for idiopathic thrombocytopenic purpura

- Aminocaproic acid
- Inhibits plasmin activation
- Methylprednisolone 1 g/d IV × 3 d
- IVIg
- Platelet transfusion
  - Given w/ IVIg or anti-Rh(D)
