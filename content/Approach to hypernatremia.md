# Approach to hypernatremia

![image 22-10-02 01 09 24](https://i.imgur.com/wSmdek6.png)

- check U_osm < 700 or > 800
  - > 800
    - U_Na < 25
    - U_Na > 100
  - < 700
    - U_osm < 300
      - Complete DI
    - U_osm 300~600
      - Partial DI, Osmotic diuresis, Loop diuretics
