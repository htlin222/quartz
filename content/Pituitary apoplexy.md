# Pituitary apoplexy

* Rapid expansion of pituitary tumor (typically adenoma) due to hemorrhage or infarction
* Sx include excruciating headache, diplopia, hypopituitarism
* Rx: immediate high-dose glucocorticoids; prompt surgical decompression if severe neurologic impairment or Î” MS; conservative management if mild
