# clinical manifestations of peptic ulcer disease

- Epigastric gnawing abdominal pain:
  - relieved with food (DU) or worsened by food (GU)
- Complic.:
  - UGIB,
  - perf. & penetration,
  - gastric outlet obstruction (due to edema & dysmotility)
