# acid-base workup TLDR

代謝性的 → 呼吸代償 = f(HCO3) → 同向跟上

- PaCO2 = (1.5 × HCO3) + 8 ± 2 ([[Winters’ formula]])
  - e.g. HCO3 = 12, PaCO2 = 26
- PaCO2 = 0.7 × (HCO3-24) + 40 ± 2 or HCO3 + 15
  - e.g. HCO3 = 36, PaCO2 = 50

> 漲五成+8 v.s. 打七折+40
