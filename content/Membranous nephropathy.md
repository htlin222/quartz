# Membranous nephropathy

(30%; CJASN 2017;12:938, JASN 2021;32:268):

## Primary

- [[Anti-PLA2R]] 70%, 
- THSD7A 5%, 
- other (EXT1-2, NELL1, Sema3B)

## Secondary

- infxn (HBV, HCV, HIV, syphilis)
- autoimmune (eg, SLE)
- carcinomas
- drugs (NSAIDs, penicillamine).
